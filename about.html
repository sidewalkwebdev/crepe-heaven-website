The Story

From Our Family
To Yours

A legacy of folding love into every bite.

It Started in Maryland.

Our journey didn't start in Lawndale—it started years ago on the East Coast.

Our parents opened the beloved Fenton Café in Silver Spring, Maryland, where they spent years perfecting the art of the crepe. We grew up in that kitchen, watching, learning, and tasting.

When we moved to the South Bay, we knew we had to bring that family tradition with us. In 2021, we opened the doors to Crepe Heaven.

The Secret Recipe.

"Is it the batter?"

That's the question we get asked the most. Why are our crepes so light, airy, and slightly sweet without being overpowering?

The answer is Mom's Secret Recipe. It's an authentic batter recipe that has been passed down and guarded carefully. We make our batter fresh from scratch every single morning. We don't use pre-made mixes. We fold every crepe to order, ensuring that whether you order savory or sweet, you're getting a slice of heaven.

Taste the Difference

More Than Just Crepes

We are a small, family-run business. When you walk in, you aren't just a customer number—you're a neighbor. We pride ourselves on creating a modern, clean, and cozy space where you can study, catch up with friends, or just enjoy a quiet coffee.
